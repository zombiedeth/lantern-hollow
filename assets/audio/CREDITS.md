# Audio Credits

Music and SFX assets used by the Godot Lantern Hollow prototype.

## Base music
- **Track:** Glowing Garden
- **Author:** mywillow (Suno)
- **Source:** https://suno.com/song/aad209d5-9729-4002-8218-c88be371593e
- **License:** Free for non-commercial use (Suno default tier)
- **File:** `music_07_glowing_garden.wav`
- **Role:** The only background music track. It replaces the old procedural base/adaptive music stems.
